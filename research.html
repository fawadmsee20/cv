4 Research Areas

Research Focus

Interdisciplinary research spanning AI, computer vision, wireless systems, and robotics.

👁️ Computer Vision & Image Processing

My work focuses on person re-identification in multi-camera surveillance systems, including both traditional feature engineering and modern deep learning approaches. I also work on 2D/3D medical image reconstruction using Variational Autoencoders (VAEs), diffusion models, and transformer-based architectures. Additional topics include texture classification, image segmentation, and object detection.

🎯

Person Re-ID

Discriminative local features, overlapping stripe models, multi-camera tracking.

🏥

Medical Imaging

Dental panoramic reconstruction from CBCT data; 3D reconstruction with VAEs and diffusion.

🌊

Texture Analysis

Low-rank multi-channel features, hybrid descriptors, polynomial approximations.

🪸

Underwater Vision

ML framework for coral localisation using bag-of-hybrid visual feature classification.

🤖 Artificial Intelligence & Deep Learning

My current postdoctoral research at KENTECH focuses on developing AI-optimised Digital Twins for anomaly detection in DC power grids. I use time-series forecasting techniques including XGBoost, GRU, Prophet, TimeGPT, and transformer models for energy systems. I also work on Visual SLAM, sensor fusion, and embedded AI for edge devices.

⚡ Wireless Power Transfer (WPT)

Designing AI-optimised WPT systems for implantable medical devices, including resonant structure design, ANSYS Maxwell electromagnetic simulations, Finite Element Analysis (FEA) optimisation, and data-driven efficiency enhancement. I also apply machine learning to jointly optimise coil and core designs for commercial WPT applications.

🚁 Indoor Localisation & UAV Navigation

Research on GPS-denied navigation for UAVs and drones using stereo vision, synchronised multi-camera setups, and robust computer-vision-based pose estimation. Indoor localisation using RSSI fingerprinting and hybrid DNN models — culminating in the RS-DeepNet framework for precise indoor positioning.
