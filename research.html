🔬 Research

My research spans the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Energy Systems. I develop data-driven, AI-optimised methods for real-world engineering challenges in smart energy grids, wireless power transfer, and autonomous navigation.

Research Interests

Computer Vision & Image Processing

Person re-identification, multi-camera surveillance, 2D/3D medical image reconstruction using VAEs, diffusion models, and transformer-based methods.

Artificial Intelligence & Deep Learning

Visual SLAM, sensor fusion, embedded AI, anomaly detection in DC grids, ML-based forecasting and optimisation with XGBoost, GRU, Prophet, TimeGPT, and transformer models.

Wireless Power Transfer (WPT)

AI-optimised WPT systems for implantable medical devices, including resonant structure design, ANSYS Maxwell simulations, FEA optimisation, and data-driven efficiency enhancement.

Indoor Localisation & UAV Navigation

RSSI fingerprinting, hybrid DNN localisation, GPS-denied UAV/drone navigation using stereo vision, synchronised multi-camera setups, and robust pose estimation.
